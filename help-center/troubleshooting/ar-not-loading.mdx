## Symptoms

- AR content stays blank or shows loading forever
- No animation appears on scan

## Possible Causes

- Camera access not granted
- Network issues

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check camera permissions">
    Make sure the browser/app is allowed to use your camera.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload and check network">
    Refresh the page and ensure you have an internet connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser or device">
