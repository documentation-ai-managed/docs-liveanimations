---
title: Create an AR Animation Project
description: Quickly build your first AR project in Liveanimations.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    Click "New Project" on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your 3D assets">
    Drag and drop GLB, OBJ, or FBX files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set AR trigger">
    Choose image or QR trigger for your animation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview & publish">
    Click "Preview" to test, then "Publish" to go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
