## Overview

To start a new project in Liveanimations:

1. Go to `https://dashboard.liveanimations.com` and sign in.
2. Click the "New Project" button.
3. Fill in your project name and description.
4. Click "Create" to start editing.

Your project will appear on your dashboard for easy access.
